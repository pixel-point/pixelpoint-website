Interhuman AI, a Copenhagen-based research lab backed by PSV Tech, is building the social intelligence layer for AI. Its model, Inter-1, detects social signals — hesitation, engagement, frustration — from video, audio, and text processed together, giving AI products the ability to respond to how people communicate, not just what they say.

The video concept was to make the social signals visible: showing a conversation the way Inter-1 sees it, with the non-verbal layer surfacing in real time alongside the words.

For a company whose entire thesis is that meaning lives beyond words, the video had to embody the message — every cut, pause, and motion choice carrying tone the script alone couldn't. That made it as much a communication-design challenge as a production one.
